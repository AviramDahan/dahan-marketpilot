# Phase 4.1: Multi-Timeframe Signal Foundation - Context

**Gathered:** 2026-06-14
**Status:** Ready for planning-only execution

<domain>
## Phase Boundary

Phase 4.1 is a product-definition and architecture foundation inserted after
Phase 4 and before Phase 5. It prepares Dahan MarketPilot for exactly three
regular strategy modes:

- `daily_only`
- `daily_filter_4h_setup`
- `daily_filter_4h_setup_1h_optional`

`daily_only` remains the default, compatibility mode, and backtesting benchmark.
Strategy mode is separate from environment modes such as `backtest`, `shadow`,
and `paper`.

This phase may add typed strategy-mode configuration, completed-bar/timeframe
models, readiness contracts, setup timing generalization, multi-resolution
QuantConnect verification notes, deterministic offline tests, and documentation.

This phase must not create production orders, Paper Trading deployment,
Telegram delivery, real Backtest execution, fake performance artifacts,
position sizing, stops, targets, portfolio mutation, or live-trading behavior.
</domain>

<decisions>
## Locked Decisions

- **D-04.1-01:** Support exactly the three regular strategy modes listed above;
  unsupported, missing, empty, or invalid modes fail closed.
- **D-04.1-02:** Keep strategy mode independent from environment mode.
- **D-04.1-03:** Preserve completed daily-bar behavior in `daily_only`.
- **D-04.1-04:** In `daily_filter_4h_setup`, Daily is mandatory context/filter
  and completed 4H bars generate the primary setup signal.
- **D-04.1-05:** In `daily_filter_4h_setup_1h_optional`, Daily and valid 4H are
  mandatory; 1H can strengthen evidence but cannot create a trade independently
  and cannot override failed Daily, invalid 4H, `RISK_OFF`, stale data, hard
  rejection, or invalid reward/risk.
- **D-04.1-06:** Emit at most one candidate per symbol. Do not create separate
  Daily, 4H, and 1H candidates.
- **D-04.1-07:** Remain swing trading with expected holding period around 3-30
  days. Do not introduce day trading, scalping, 5m/15m/HFT behavior, or
  mandatory same-day exits.
- **D-04.1-08:** Signals must use completed bars only and must never use future
  or incomplete bars, future context, or unrealistic same-bar assumptions.
- **D-04.1-09:** Generalize `SetupTiming` around completed-bar metadata rather
  than hardcoding daily-only timing.
- **D-04.1-10:** Backtesting weights and MTF scoring weights are not finalized
  before backtesting and sensitivity validation.
</decisions>

<timeframe_contracts>
## Timeframe Responsibilities

Daily is mandatory in all modes. It handles universe eligibility, liquidity,
data quality, broad trend, EMA structure, SPY/QQQ regime, broad relative
strength, volatility, gap and earnings-risk context, and rejection of
structurally weak candidates.

4H is the primary setup/signal timeframe in MTF modes. It handles Trend
Pullback setup quality, Volume Breakout setup quality, recovery or breakout
confirmation, momentum/volume evidence, invalidation, and primary setup
timestamp.

1H is optional confirmation only in
`daily_filter_4h_setup_1h_optional`. It can improve confidence, readiness, and
supporting evidence. Missing 1H alone cannot reject a valid Daily+4H candidate.
</timeframe_contracts>

<recommendation>
## Recommended Architecture

Use a typed `config/strategy.yaml` strategy-mode contract with `daily_only` as
the default. Add reusable timeframe-aware models such as `BarTimeframe`,
`CompletedBar`, `BarCompletionStatus`, and `BarSessionMetadata`. Final class
names may change during implementation if they fit existing code better.

Recommended 4H policy: market-open anchored regular-hours bars in
`America/New_York`, extended-hours excluded, DST/holiday/early-close aware.
Because a regular US equity session is 6.5 hours and does not divide evenly
into 4H, the first full 4H bar may be signal-eligible and the remaining
partial session must be explicitly marked partial and non-signal by default.
The partial bar may be retained as non-signal evidence only after tests prove
it cannot create look-ahead or same-bar ambiguity. Evaluate a 2H alternative
only if 4H proves unsafe or too sparse; do not silently replace 4H.
</recommendation>
